The Reptiles

Reptiles come in many varieties. They can be the snakes that roam the forests or the giant snakes in the marshes of Sosaria. They are the lizardmen that have built civilizations on the outskirts of lakes and bogs. Or they are the majestic and highly intelligent dragons that soar through the air or guard the vast caves full of treasure. Some have found ancient portals that transport you to long-forgotten dragon roosts. As you adventure through Sosaria it is best to be prepared for these creatures at all times. In the caves and lairs of great beasts, you will find the dragons of Sosaria. They can be the infant drakes, young dragons, normal dragons, or the ancient wyrms of myth. Some adventurers claim to have found ancient guardians throughout the land that protect vast amounts of knowledge, relics, and eggs of their kind. If it is the riches of these beasts you are after then it is best to prepare yourself with the correct weapons and armor. It is said that the runes of punt etched into armor will protect you from reptiles. The finest reptile slayer weapons can be crafted by master blacksmiths and will make quick work of all reptiles you may come across.
